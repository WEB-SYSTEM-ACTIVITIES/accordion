INFORMATION MANAGEMENT

o Information management is an emerging field that is concerned with: The infrastructure used to collect, manage, preserve, store and deliver information.

NETWORKING 1

o Computer networking refers to interconnected computing devices that can exchange data and share resources with each other. These networked devices use a system of rules, called communications protocols, to transmit information over physical or wireless technologies.

WEB SYSTEMS AND TECHNOLOGIES

o Web information system, or web-based information system, is an information system that uses Internet web technologies to deliver information and services, to users or other information systems/applications.

SYSTEMS INTEGRATION AND ARCHITECTURE 1

o The Systems Integration Architecture provides a bridge between the heterogeneous operational applications and platforms. This architecture ties together the mix of platforms, operating systems, transports, and applications.
